About

Tal Levi has been practicing makeup artistry since 2011, although had known from a young age that makeup was her calling. Tal completed her makeup artistry training at Il Makiage Makeup Academy. Today, Tal specializes in bridal makeup. Her services include arriving to the venue of the bride's choice, allowing the bride to prepare in a calm and unhurried atmosphere. In addition, Tal provides professional makeup services for bridesmaids, women with mature skin, and any women who simply wish to look their best. She uses makeup in a fashion that is uniquely hers, using quality products that ensure a perfect look that will remain securely in place throughout the day. Tal practices makeup with a great love for the field.
